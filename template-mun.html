LETTER FROM THE

SECRETARY-GENERAL

Distinguished delegates, advisors, and guests,

As the Secretary-General of AegisMUN'26, it is my distinct honor to welcome you to this year's iteration of our Model United Nations simulation, taking place from August 27th to August 29th.

Our academic and organizing teams have worked meticulously to construct a platform that facilitates deep debate, compromises, and innovative solutions. In an era marked by rapid technological evolution and complex geopolitical shifts, our selected committees aim to push you to think critically, communicate strategically, and embody the highest virtues of international diplomacy.

Whether you are stepping into a committee room for the first time or are an experienced delegate navigating a fast-paced crisis, AegisMUN'26 offers a unique environment to test your negotiation skills and develop collective answers to our world's most pressing challenges.

We look forward to witnessing your academic rigor and collaborative spirit. Welcome to AegisMUN'26.

"Diplomacy is the art of letting someone else have your way."
